Background

The Arc

Airlines Telecom Finance Media Higher Ed Healthcare

Started in 1997 building airline reservation systems—mainframes, manual failovers, the kind of systems where downtime meant stranded passengers. Moved through telecom during the scale era, finance during the compliance crackdowns, media when everything went digital.


Now in healthcare, where the stakes are different but the patterns rhyme. HIPAA, FHIR, EMR integration—different acronyms, same fundamental challenges of moving data safely between systems that weren't designed to talk to each other.


What 27 years teaches you isn't a stack or a framework. It's pattern recognition. The ability to see a problem and remember when you saw something similar fail—and why.

The Senior + AI Formula

Why experience is the force multiplier.

AI generates code; wisdom generates architecture. In an era of infinite syntax generation, the value of knowing what to build has never been higher. I leverage LLMs to accelerate execution, but architectural integrity remains human-led. The result is senior-level output at startup speed—not despite the 27 years, but because of them.
